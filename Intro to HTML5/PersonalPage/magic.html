Magic:

Besides music, magic has been one of my longest and most concentrated studies (well, besides University work).

I think people would truly be surprised by not only the history, but the amount of information related to teh subject. I personally have spent thousands of dollars in building up my "magic book" library and it doesn't even look like much.

Too often non-magicians think of magicians in this either clown-like or creepy type of way. This isn't anyone's fault however. Magicians have made a mockery of themselves for the last 50 years and it is only now starting to change back to a performance based art. Magic is not an art within itself, but there are some that can elivate it to a new level.

I have been creating my own tricks for years. My main area of study is sleight of hand with cards or coins, but I enjoy coming upu with creative solutions to something that seems impossible. Some of the smartest people in the world are magicians. Seriously...
