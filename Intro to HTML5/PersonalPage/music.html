Music:

Music holds a lot of importance in my life. When I was 21 I joined a band that was determined to make it. I sacrificed for years of my life, selling my vehicle for a trailer for tour, giving up any career oppourtunities right after I gradudated high school, working minimum-wage jobs to pay my bills and buy better equipment (while not worrying if I just quit), going into tens of thousands of dollars in debt, etc. I love music.

I play guitar and I have an ESP Eclipse Silverburst and a Gibson Les Paul studio that I wouldn't trade for the world. Life would be pretty damn dull without music.
